BMI Calculator

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. The formula is BMI = kg/m² where kg is a person's weight in kilograms and m² is their height in meters squared. It is used to categorize individuals into underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity, helping to assess their overall health.
